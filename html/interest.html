Passions as a Creator

Automation

From homes to businesses, automation has become a central theme in our lives. This is for good reason, it makes our jobs and lives easier. As a Software Engineer, it is my job to use automation as a tool to help businesses and everyday individuals live a more productive life without the need for repetitive processes. Personally, I use these automation tools to build devices and networks that make my home more productive.

Embedded Systems

Thanks to the help of embedded computers and systems, technology today has progressed far past what could have been imagined ten years ago. The two most popular forms of embedded systems come as raspberry pi and arduino, both of which I have experience using. I enjoy creating devices which use embedded systems in order to help the main device function efficiently.

Alternative Interests

Although embedded systems and automation are two areas I deeply enjoy learning about, I have other interests including: artificial intelligence, web development, cyber security, data science, and programming languages.
